Background

Attorney Paul B. Stanton is one of Massachusetts's most dedicated and trusted criminal defense attorneys. With over 25 years of litigation experience, his commitment to your rights and the legal system is simply unshakable.

He has been providing comprehensive and easy-to-understand counsel to the people of Massachusetts since 2000. He understands how overwhelming the legal process can be, and his goal is to help you understand your rights and the issues affecting your case.

A Former Prosecutor's Advantage

Before dedicating his career to criminal defense, Paul served as a Prosecuting Attorney at the Norfolk County District Attorney's Office. This experience gave him invaluable insight into how the prosecution investigates, builds, and presents its cases.

Now, he uses that knowledge to your advantage. By understanding the strategies and tactics of the other side, Paul is able to anticipate the prosecution's moves and build a stronger defense for every client he represents.

Practice Areas

Attorney Stanton focuses his practice on the areas where his experience can make the greatest impact:

  • Criminal Defense — Felonies, misdemeanors, drug offenses, assault, theft, and all criminal charges
  • DUI & DWI Defense — OUI defense including breathalyzer challenges and field sobriety test disputes
  • Speeding & Traffic Violations — License protection and charge reduction or dismissal
  • Personal Injury — Representing those injured through the negligence of others

Commitment to Clients

Paul believes that everyone deserves a strong defense and clear communication throughout the legal process. He takes the time to explain each step, answer every question, and ensure that his clients feel informed and empowered about the decisions that affect their lives.

Whether your case requires negotiation or aggressive courtroom advocacy, Attorney Stanton has the skill, experience, and determination to fight for the best possible outcome.
