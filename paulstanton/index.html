About the Attorney

A Former Prosecutor
Fighting For You

Attorney Paul B. Stanton has been providing comprehensive and easy-to-understand counsel to the people of Massachusetts since 2000. He understands how overwhelming the legal process can be, and his goal is to help you understand your rights and the issues affecting your case.

As a former Prosecuting Attorney at the Norfolk County District Attorney's Office, Paul brings invaluable insight into how the prosecution builds its cases — and how to dismantle them.
